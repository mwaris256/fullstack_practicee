I grew up playing and loving cricket in Pakistan. I used to play with my cousins and friends. I was always a good batsman in tape ball cricket but I have not been able to convert those tape ball performances into hardball cricket.

Unlike my batting performances, I have not been able to improve my bowling. I have tried to bowl pace and even tried spin bowling but I bowl too many wides and get smacked for too many fours and sixes. Hence, I have given up on bowling and begun to focus more on my batting

I have always been a good fielder. I have taken some really good catches since a young age. I would practice catching with my friends in school at lunch time. In my highschool's cricket team, I chose to be a wicket keeper and I am also a parttime wicket keeper for my hardball cricket team. Although, I am a good wicket keeper I feel like I can improve my keeping against spin bowling.
